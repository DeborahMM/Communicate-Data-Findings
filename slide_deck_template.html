2018 Ford GoBike System Data¶

by Deborah Micael¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, the behavior of subscribers and customers was analyzed. The main focus was on the daily, hourly, and monthly travel distance and duration between customers and subscribers.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area (San Francisco, East Bay, San Jose). This dataset requires some data wrangling in order to make it tidy for analysis. There are multiple data files that will need to be joined together for a full year’s coverage. The combined 2018 Ford GoBike System Data contains 1,863,721 trips. 156,703 points were removed from the analysis due to inconsistencies.

User Type¶

An overwhelming number of users are subscribers (87.7%).

Trip Duration vs Trip Distance¶

There is no correlation between trip distance and trip duration which may be due to the use of both electric and regular bikes.

Monthly Bike Ride Trend¶

Overall, the monthly bike rides for customers increase then plateaus in May and then decreases. However, the monthly bike rides gradually increases and plateaues, peaking in October, and followed by a dramatic drop in November and December.

Hourly Bike Ride Trend¶

The hourly bike ride trend is binomial with peaks at 8 AM and 7 PM. However, the peaks are much more pronounced for subscribers than that of customers.

Daily Bike Ride Trend¶

The weekend is popular for customers unlike subscribers. Moreover, the weekend is the least popular time for subscribers. Tuesday is the most popular, followed by Wednesday and Thursday among the subscribers. Saturday is the most popular day, followed by Friday and Sunday among the Customers.

Average Monthly Trip Distance¶

The average monthly trip distance is similar, but customers have slightly higher averages.

Average Hourly Trip Distance¶

The average hourly trip distance is similar, but customers have slightly higher averages.

Average Daily Trip Distance¶

The average daily trip distance is similar, but customers have slightly higher averages even during the weekend.

Most Frequently Used Daily Trip Durations During Peak Hours¶

The most frequently used trips last 5-10 minutes during the weekdays among customers and subscribers. Moreover, customers have similar trip durations to subscribers during the weekend although customers tend to be the most active at that time.

Most Frequently Used Monthly Trip Durations During Peak Hours¶

The most frequently used trips last 5-10 minutes from April through October among customers and subscribers.

Most Frequently Used Daily Trip Distance During Peak Hours¶

The most frequently used trips are 0.5-1 km during the weekdays among customers and subscribers. Moreover, customers have similar trip distances to subscribers during the weekend although customers tend to be the most active at that time.
